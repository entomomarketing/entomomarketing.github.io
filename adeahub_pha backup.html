The Challenge

Patients miss follow-up appointments when post-discharge reminders depend on staff to chase them.

Inbound scheduling calls consume clinical administration time that should be directed toward patient care.

Patients discharged without structured guidance are significantly more likely to return as avoidable readmissions.

Without a direct digital channel, your hospital has no presence in your patients' lives between visits.

When patients cannot easily access their care between visits, clinical outcomes suffer and operational costs rise. Health systems that close this gap with a trusted, provider-owned patient app see measurable improvements in adherence, satisfaction, and appointment attendance.

How AH Health App Helps Your Team

For Clinical Leaders

CMO · CMIO · Clinical Director

Structured post-discharge care plans, medication management, and vital signs monitoring keep patients on protocol between visits and reduce readmission risk — without adding to your clinical team's workload. In-app messaging and telemedicine let your team handle follow-ups remotely and efficiently.

For IT Leaders

CIO · CTO · VP Applications

AH Health App integrates with your existing HIS and EMR through standard HL7 and FHIR interfaces. Modular architecture and provider-controlled data governance give your IT team confidence from day one.

For Administrators

CFO · VP Operations · Administrator

Fewer no-shows, lower call-centre volume, and reduced per-patient administrative cost. Virtual queuing shortens wait times without adding front-desk staff. The in-app marketplace and loyalty programme create new digital revenue streams. Digital billing and insurance claim management cuts collection cycles.

Challenge:

The UAE Ministry of Health and Prevention needed a digital platform to connect patients across the country during a national public health emergency.

adeahub deployed the Reaya patient app in partnership with MOHAP, Du Telecom, and Emirates Health Services — enabling remote monitoring, geo-fenced movement tracking, and real-time case management at a national level.

Result:

The platform served patients across all UAE governorates, integrated with law enforcement dashboards and MOHAP systems, and was recognised with the Gold Award at the Stevie Awards MENA 2021 for excellence in technology-driven healthcare innovation.
