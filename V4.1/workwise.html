The challenge

Healthcare organisations run on people — but most are managing their workforce with systems designed for other industries. Scheduling a 24/7 nurse roster manually. Tracking credentials in spreadsheets. Setting performance goals that bear no relation to clinical outcomes. Running annual reviews that arrive too late to prevent a good nurse from walking out the door.

The result: high turnover in critical units, scheduling gaps that create unsafe patient ratios, and a workforce that feels managed rather than valued — directly impacting the quality of patient care.

What WorkWise does

WorkWise by entomo replaces the patchwork of disconnected HR tools with a single, healthcare-specific workforce experience platform. Performance goals are built from a healthcare KPI library and connected to clinical outcomes. Scheduling is AI-assisted, fatigue-aware, and compliant. Career pathways are visible, personalised, and actively managed. Wellness is monitored daily — not once at exit.

The five workforce barriers WorkWise addresses

High turnover in critical units

Nursing attrition is highest in ICU, ED, and oncology — the units where continuity matters most. WorkWise surfaces retention risks before they become resignations.

24/7 scheduling complexity

Manual rosters across specialties, wards, and shifts create scheduling gaps, overtime exposure, and compliance violations. AI-assisted scheduling eliminates the guesswork.

Credential and compliance gaps

Tracking licences, certifications, and mandatory training across hundreds of staff manually is error-prone and audit-risky. WorkWise automates the entire credential lifecycle.

Burnout without early warning

By the time burnout shows in turnover data, the damage is done. WorkWise captures daily wellness pulses and sends proactive alerts to managers — weeks earlier.

Disconnected HR systems

Performance goals in one system. Scheduling in another. Learning in a third. WorkWise unifies every workforce touchpoint — so the experience of every clinician is connected, not fragmented.

The vision

One platform that understands what a great healthcare worker needs to thrive.

Healthcare workforce management is unlike any other industry. Clinical staff operate under 24/7 shift patterns, carry credential and compliance obligations that expire, and face the kind of emotional and physical demands that lead to burnout faster than in almost any other profession.

WorkWise brings every workforce function into a single, deeply integrated platform — built from the ground up for healthcare workflows, and powered by an AI layer that coaches, nudges, and guides at the individual level.

From the moment a new nurse onboards through to the moment a senior clinician is considered for a leadership pathway, every touchpoint is connected, personalised, and visible to the right people at the right time.

What it covers

Six workforce domains. One integrated experience.

WorkWise covers the full spectrum of healthcare workforce management — from shift scheduling through to career pathways and AI-powered coaching.

Performance & Goal Intelligence

A healthcare-specific KPI library translates clinical outcomes into individual goals. AI nudges keep every staff member on track — without waiting for the annual review cycle to surface problems.

Smart Scheduling

AI-assisted shift allocation that optimises for coverage, fatigue prevention, and skill mix across every ward and specialty. Automates swap management and flags compliance violations before they occur.

Career Development & Learning

Digital individual development plans (IDPs), career pathway mapping, and skill profiling — so every clinician can see a clear path forward and every manager can identify and grow future leaders.

Wellness & Burnout Prevention

Daily wellness pulse checks and mid-day check-ins capture sentiment in real time. Proactive alerts reach managers weeks before burnout escalates to attrition — connecting staff wellbeing directly to patient outcomes.

Compliance & Credentials

Automated tracking of licences, certifications, and mandatory training across your entire clinical workforce. Incident reporting workflows and payroll summaries complete the compliance picture — reducing audit risk and administrative burden.

WorkWise AI — Agentic Layer

GoalSense AI, PerformWise AI, and GrowthPath AI form a team of specialised agents that coach, nudge, and guide every staff member — operating autonomously within the platform to surface the right insight to the right person at the right moment.

WorkWise AI

A team of AI agents working for every person in your workforce.

WorkWise AI is entomo's agentic intelligence layer — specialised AI agents that operate autonomously to coach, nudge, and guide clinical staff and managers, at scale.

GoalSense AI

Goal Intelligence Agent

Translates healthcare KPIs into personalised, achievable goals for each staff member. Monitors progress, sends timely nudges, and surfaces early warning signals when a goal is at risk — without waiting for manager intervention.

PerformWise AI

Performance Coach Agent

Delivers continuous, personalised performance coaching to clinical staff — replacing the annual review with an always-on feedback loop aligned to clinical outcomes and individual development goals.

GrowthPath AI

Career Coach Agent

Maps each clinician's skills and experience to available career pathways — proactively surfacing opportunities, recommending learning modules, and guiding individual development plans aligned to the organisation's talent strategy.

Feedback360 AI

Performance Feedback Agent

Orchestrates structured 360-degree feedback across clinical teams — gathering peer, manager, and self-assessments, synthesising them into actionable insights, and delivering them at the right moment in the performance cycle.

LearnPath AI

IDP & Learning Agent

Automates the creation and management of individual learning pathways — matching each staff member's development goals to the right content, mandatory training requirements, and skill gaps identified through performance data.

SkillPassport AI

Skill Profiling Agent

Builds and maintains a dynamic skill profile for every staff member — tracking certifications, clinical competencies, and learning completions in real time, and connecting talent supply to organisational demand across specialties.
