Infrastructure Layer

Most health systems don't have an interoperability problem — they have a fragmentation problem. Dozens of disconnected systems, each holding a piece of the patient record, none of them designed to work together. adeahub exists to resolve that — not by replacing your existing infrastructure, but by becoming the coherent layer that connects it.

Built on internationally recognised open standards — FHIR R4, HL7 v2/v3, SNOMED CT, ICD-10, and LOINC — adeahub integrates with legacy hospital information systems, third-party diagnostic platforms, national health registries, and government interoperability frameworks without requiring proprietary lock-in. Health systems in 18 countries have deployed adeahub into live clinical and administrative environments, managing over 200 million patient records across 500+ facilities.

The platform scales from a 100-bed district hospital to a national-level EHR programme. It runs in public cloud, private cloud, and on-premise environments. It supports real-time and batch data exchange via REST APIs, XML, and JSON. And it is maintained by a team with 25+ years of hands-on implementation experience in the markets it serves.

Embedded Intelligence
adea AI

Intelligence embedded in every clinical workflow.

adea AI isn't a chatbot bolted onto a dashboard. It runs inside the platform — surfacing the right insight, at the right moment, for the right role, without interrupting the workflows clinicians and administrators already rely on.

Clinical Decision Support

Embedded in AH Care

Evidence-based alerts and order-set recommendations surface at the point of care — inside the physician's existing workflow. Drug interaction warnings, dosing checks, and protocol guidance are triggered in real time, reducing clinical errors without adding documentation burden.

Predictive Risk Stratification

Embedded in AH Insights

AI models score patient risk across deterioration, readmission, and chronic disease progression — before a clinical event occurs. High-risk patients are surfaced to care teams automatically, enabling proactive intervention rather than reactive treatment.

Automated Triage & Alerts

Embedded in AH Care — ER

Intelligent triage scoring in the Emergency Department prioritises patients by acuity and flags deteriorating cases to the responsible clinical team in real time. Critical lab values and vital sign anomalies trigger immediate alerts — no manual monitoring required.

Population Health Modelling

Embedded in AH Insights

Longitudinal patient data across facilities is aggregated and modelled to identify disease trends, high-burden cohorts, and intervention opportunities at the population level — giving health authorities and hospital groups the intelligence to plan and allocate resources ahead of demand.

Intelligent Documentation

Embedded in AH Care — EMR

AI-assisted clinical documentation reduces the time clinicians spend on note entry — auto-populating structured fields from prior encounters, suggesting ICD codes from free-text descriptions, and flagging documentation gaps before discharge to support accurate billing and coding.

Claims & Denial Intelligence

Embedded in AH Care — RCM

AI monitors claim submissions in real time, predicting denial risk before submission and routing high-risk claims for review. Denial patterns are analysed across payers to surface systemic coding or documentation issues — reducing rejection rates and protecting collections at scale.

One Platform. Four Integrated Products.

Care for your patients. Engage with them beyond the hospital. Connect across your ecosystem. And let Insights guide every decision.

Clinical Core

AH Care

An enterprise-class Health Information System covering end-to-end clinical and administrative operations. 25+ integrated modules span registration, CPOE, ER, OR, pharmacy, laboratory, radiology, nursing, and billing — all on a single database. HIMSS Stage 7-validated workflows ensure that clinicians get structured, accurate data at the point of care, in the system they are already using.

Patient Engagement

AH Engage

A white-label Personal Health App that extends care beyond the hospital. Patients access appointments, health records, care plans, and follow-up instructions from a single hospital-branded portal — on any device. Built on the same infrastructure that powered MySejahtera, ranked #1 worldwide for install penetration and open rate among COVID-19 apps by Data.ai in 2021.

Interoperability Engine

AH Connect

A vendor-neutral interoperability platform with a built-in FHIR exchange server, versatile integration engine, and smart data fabric. Connects national health stacks, PACS, LIS, ERP, wearables, and third-party applications in real time or batch. Deployed in Brunei's nationwide One-Patient One-Record programme — linking 4 hospitals and 60+ health centres across 433,000 citizens.

Analytics & Intelligence

AH Insights

A population health and analytics layer providing executive KPI dashboards, real-time clinical alerts, and AI/ML models for patient risk stratification. Geo-spatial visualisation supports communicable disease management and regional health planning. Retrospective and prospective analytics run on the same normalised data platform as the clinical and operational systems.

Built on Open Healthcare Standards

adeahub natively supports FHIR STU3 and R4, HL7 v2 and v3, SNOMED, ICD-10, NANDA, and CPT. The integration engine handles HL7, XML, JSON, FHIR, REST APIs, EDI, and proprietary flat-file formats — uni- or bi-directional, real-time or batch.

Pre-built connectors are available for PACS, LIS, ERP, smart card systems, national health registries, drug databases, and SMS/email gateways. API documentation is available upon request for technical evaluation teams.

Enterprise-Grade Security

adeahub is built on a Zero Trust security architecture with data governance and observability across every platform layer. Patient data is protected through role-based access controls, comprehensive audit logging, and secure data exchange over encrypted channels.

Deployment configurations — whether cloud, on-premise, or hybrid — do not compromise the security posture. Clinical workflow standards are independently validated against HIMSS EMRAM and JCI accreditation benchmarks.

Security Architecture

Zero Trust Architecture

No implicit trust between any layer or service. Every access request is verified.

Role-Based Access Controls
