National Programme

Universal Health Insurance Authority (UHIA)

Egypt
Type
National Health Authority
Scale
350 facilities · 6 governorates · 5M+ beneficiaries
Solutions Used
adeahub providers

Digital transformation at national scale: 350 facilities, 5 million patients, zero paper.

Challenge: Egypt's UHIA needed to unify clinical records across 350 healthcare facilities in 6 governorates — eliminating paper workflows and enabling continuity of care for over 5 million insured beneficiaries.

Solution: adeahub providers delivered a single-instance EMR across all facilities, with standards-based integrations to lab, radiology, and payor systems. Online appointment booking, digital order entry, and medications management were deployed across the full network.

Result: Complete paperless transformation across 6 governorates. Over 5 million patients now have a unified electronic medical record. Internal and external referrals are fully digital.

Multi-Facility Hospital Network

Kokilaben Dhirubhai Ambani Hospital (KDAH)

India
Type
Tertiary Care Hospital Group
Scale
3 hospitals · 1,250 beds · Andheri, Navi Mumbai & Indore
Solutions Used
adeahub providers

Three hospitals. One unified patient record. Continuous care across Mumbai and Indore.

Challenge: KDAH needed a single platform to manage clinical and administrative operations across three hospitals — including two NABH-accredited Mumbai campuses — serving high patient volumes across diverse specialties while supporting medical tourism.

Solution: adeahub providers was deployed as a central single-database system, giving all three hospitals access to a unified Master Patient Index and continuous electronic medical record. The platform integrates with PACS, 50+ lab analysers, SAP ERP, and a video conferencing appointment system.

Result: Active and uninterrupted since 2011, with contract renewals secured through 2027. Clinicians across all three facilities operate from one connected system, with no data silos between campuses.

National Ministry of Health Programme

Ministry of Health Malaysia

Malaysia
Type
National Ministry of Health
Scale
10+ hospitals · 100 to 1,200 beds per site · Nationwide
Solutions Used
adeahub providers

A scalable national HIS — from community hospitals to 1,200-bed tertiary centres.

Challenge: Malaysia's Ministry of Health required a single, scalable hospital information system deployable across facilities of widely varying sizes — from basic community hospitals to large tertiary hospitals — with a roadmap toward a nationwide electronic health record.

Solution: adeahub providers was deployed across multiple hospital tiers, each configured to the right module depth. The platform spans appointment scheduling, inpatient and outpatient management, clinical documentation, pharmacy, lab, radiology, and integration to national health information systems including MyHIX.

Result: adeahub now powers MoH Malaysia's hospital network across multiple states, with the most recent deployments at Serdang (1,200 beds) and Sungai Buloh (1,200 beds) going live in 2023–24. The programme supports Malaysia's long-term ambition for a nationally connected health record.
