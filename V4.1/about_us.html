Our Story

How we got here.

We saw a pattern repeating itself across health systems in Asia, the Middle East, and Africa. Patients were moving between clinics, hospitals, and specialists — but their health data was not. The result was duplicated tests, delayed decisions, and care that felt fragmented to everyone delivering it, and everyone receiving it.

Our roots go back to 1998, when the regional healthcare IT lineage that adeahub is built on was first established across Asia, the Middle East, and Africa. Over the following two decades, we built deep expertise in large-scale clinical informatics and hospital information systems — expertise that reached a defining moment in 2020 when we deployed MySejahtera, Malaysia's national COVID-19 platform, which reached 35 million users. In 2025, we acquired the Asia, Middle East, and Africa operations of Dedalus Healthcare, adding over two decades of HIS implementation depth to our foundation. adeahub was built on that heritage — unifying provider systems, national health infrastructure, and patient-facing platforms into one coherent experience.

Today, our platform manages over 200 million patient records across 500+ healthcare facilities. We are proud of that scale. But every number represents a patient whose care journey we helped make more connected.

Mission, Vision & Values

Why we exist, and what we stand for.

Our Mission

To enable public health agencies and healthcare networks to deliver better patient care — through modern, connected digital platforms that give clinicians, administrators, and patients a single source of truth.

Our Vision

A world where a patient's health data follows them — across clinics, hospitals, and borders — so that every clinical decision is informed, every care journey is continuous, and no patient falls through the gap.

Our Core Values

Patient First

Every product decision starts with one question: does this make care better for the patient at the end of the journey?

Evidence Over Hype

We measure success in clinical outcomes and operational results — not capability lists or feature announcements.

Open by Design

We build on open standards so health data can flow freely and securely to where patients and providers need it most.

Clinically Rigorous

Our workflows are built to clinical standards, validated in real health systems, and trusted by clinicians who use them daily.

Partners, Not Vendors

We invest in long-term relationships. Your implementation success is ours. We stay long after go-live.
