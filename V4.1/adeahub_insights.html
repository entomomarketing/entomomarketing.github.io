Case Study

King Abdullah University Hospital

Jordan — BI Analytics & KPI Dashboard Platform

Enterprise analytics deployment
The challenge

King Abdullah University Hospital needed a unified analytics layer that could draw clinical, operational, and financial data from disparate systems into a single, coherent view. Hospital leaders were making decisions based on delayed reports and fragmented data — limiting their ability to respond to capacity pressures, clinician workload imbalances, and service quality trends in time to act.

What we did

adeahub deployed AH Insights as the hospital's central analytics platform — delivering over 200 standardised KPIs across clinical, operational, and financial domains. The platform connected to existing EMR and HIS systems through FHIR, HL7, and REST APIs, and provided real-time dashboards, ad-hoc reporting, and geo-spatial visualisation for hospital leadership and department heads.

The vision

Healthcare decisions delayed are outcomes missed.

Most hospital leaders still make decisions based on reports that are days old, drawn from systems that don't speak to each other. By the time a problem is visible, the window to act has closed. AH Insights gives every decision-maker — from the CEO to the clinical director to the operations manager — a real-time, unified picture of performance across every facility and every function.

Not because data is interesting. Because the right decision, made in time, changes a patient's outcome.

What it covers

Intelligence built for every layer of the organisation

AH Insights is modular by design — delivering the right view to the right person, from the board to the bedside.

Executive dashboards

Real-time KPIs covering revenue, bed occupancy, patient flow, and clinician load — giving hospital leadership a single source of truth for strategic and operational decisions.

Clinician workload intelligence

Analytics dashboards that track clinician workload distribution, caseload imbalances, and early burnout risk indicators — helping administrators respond before staff are overstretched.

Service line planning

Strategic dashboards for hospital leadership and service line directors — tracking department performance, demand trends, and resource utilisation to support long-term planning decisions.

Cross-facility benchmarking

Standardised metrics across all connected facilities enable performance comparison, trend detection, and best-practice identification at the network or group level.

Predictive analytics

Rule-based and machine learning models that surface clinical and operational risks before they materialise — enabling proactive interventions rather than reactive responses.

Geo-spatial visualisation

Population-level mapping for public health teams and multi-facility operators — visualising care access gaps, disease prevalence, and programme performance across a geographic area.

Who it's for

The right intelligence, for every decision-maker

AH Insights adapts to the needs of each user — from the board room to the department level.

Hospital leadership

CEO · COO · CFO · CMO

A single dashboard combining financial performance, bed utilisation, and care quality metrics. Leaders can identify operational bottlenecks, track improvement initiatives, and make resourcing decisions with real-time confidence — without waiting for weekly reports.

Clinical leaders

CMIO · Clinical directors · Department heads

Workload analytics showing which clinicians are overloaded, which departments are underperforming, and where care quality metrics are trending in the wrong direction. Early signals before they become serious problems — with the context to act.

Operations managers

VP Operations · Department directors · Practice managers

Operational dashboards tracking patient flow, wait times, resource utilisation, and staff productivity. AH Insights replaces manual spreadsheet consolidation with automated, standardised reporting — freeing managers to act, not aggregate.

Health authority officials

Ministry of Health · Public health departments

Population-level dashboards for national and regional health programmes. Geo-spatial analytics, cross-facility performance benchmarking, and public health surveillance metrics — supporting evidence-based policy decisions and intervention planning at scale.
