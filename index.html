The platform

Enterprise-grade. Open standards. Built to last.

adeahub is a unified digital health platform built on open standards — FHIR, HL7, ICD, and REST APIs. A single codebase scales from a primary health centre to a national EHR, with centralised, decentralised, and hybrid deployment supported across cloud and on-premise infrastructure. It connects to existing EHRs, national health stacks, devices, and third-party systems without replacing them.

Zero Trust security, data governance, and an active half-yearly release cycle are built into the foundation. 35M+ users across the world are on adeahub platform today.
