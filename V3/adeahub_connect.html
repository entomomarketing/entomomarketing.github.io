Case Study

Egypt Universal Health Insurance

National HIE & Digital Care Platform — DC4H

National-scale HIE deployment
The challenge

Egypt's Universal Health Insurance programme required a single, unified digital health infrastructure connecting 321 facilities across 6 governorates — integrating disparate hospitals, clinics, labs, and pharmacies into a coherent care record for every enrolled citizen. Without a robust integration layer, patient data would remain siloed and the programme's clinical and financial objectives could not be met.

What we did

adeahub deployed AH Connect as the DC4H (Digital Care for Health) integration backbone — delivering a full Health Information Exchange (HIE), FHIR server, API connector layer, Integrated Care Record, and BI Analytics workbench. Working alongside Vodafone Egypt under a landmark national contract, the platform connected every facility into a single, queryable patient record — regardless of the underlying system at each site.

What it covers

Six integration components. One coherent health data layer.

AH Connect provides every component needed to connect, normalise, and govern health data across your entire network.

FHIR Server

A fully compliant FHIR R4 repository — the single source of truth for your population's health records. Queryable by any authorised system or application, in real time.

Integration Engine

Bidirectional data routing between any combination of systems using 40+ connector protocols — HL7, FHIR, REST, JDBC, Cassandra, MongoDB, FTP/SFTP, JMS, and TCP/IP. Handles CSV, XML, JSON, EDI, and binary formats natively.

Enterprise Master Patient Index (EMPI)

Intelligent patient matching across all connected systems — resolving duplicate identities and ensuring every record links to a single, verified patient identity across your network.

API Gateway

A secure, managed API layer exposing your health data to authorised third-party applications, patient apps, and government systems — with full access control, rate limiting, and audit logging.

Analytics Workbench

Pre-built KPI dashboards and geo-spatial visualisation tools drawing from the integrated data lake — enabling operational and population health reporting across your entire network from day one.

Integrated Care Record

A longitudinal, patient-centric view of every clinical event across all connected facilities — giving clinicians a complete picture regardless of where the patient was seen or which system documented the encounter.

Technical foundation

Zero Trust Security

AH Connect supports inbound data from any healthcare source — database listeners for JDBC, Cassandra, and MongoDB; file listeners for FTP/SFTP; messaging listeners for HL7, HTTP/HTTPS, JMS, and TCP/IP. Outbound data is delivered in any required format: CSV, XML, HL7, FHIR R4, JSON, EDI, or binary. The result is a single integration platform capable of connecting any combination of systems, at any scale.
