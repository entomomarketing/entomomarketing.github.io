The challenge

Malaysia's public health network — spanning 8,000+ facilities and 33 million citizens — had no unified digital front door. Patients navigated separate systems, missed appointments through no fault of their own, and had no direct connection to their care team between visits.

What we did

adeahub deployed the national patient platform, integrating it across the full network of public health facilities — enabling appointment booking, health records access, vaccination management, and care navigation at a scale no commercial health app had achieved in the region.

The vision

Your hospital in every patient's pocket.

A decade ago, patients called a hospital to book an appointment, visited a counter to collect results, and had no way to reach their care team between visits.

AH Engage changes that. It gives every patient a branded digital front door to their hospital — available on every smartphone, integrated with every system, always connected.

The same way personal banking eliminated the need to visit a branch for routine transactions, AH Engage eliminates the friction between patients and the care they need.

What it covers

Six capability domains. One unified patient experience.

AH Engage covers every dimension of patient engagement — fully modular, fully white-labelled under your hospital brand.

Appointments & Queuing

Book, reschedule, and check real-time wait times — directly from the app, integrated with your HIS for live availability.

Records & Results

Access clinical documents and lab results as soon as they are verified — securely, instantly, and without a front-desk call.

Care Plans & Follow-Up

Structured post-visit journeys with medication reminders, task checklists, and progress tracking assigned at the point of discharge.

Telemedicine & Messaging

Video consultations and secure messaging with your care team — built in, with no third-party app required.

Chronic Care & Monitoring

Patient-logged vitals and structured monitoring pathways for diabetes, hypertension, and other ongoing conditions.

Marketplace & Billing

On-demand health services, in-app payments, and insurance claim management — creating new digital revenue for your organisation.

Who it's for

Every stakeholder. One platform. Clear value.

AH Engage speaks to clinical, technical, and financial leaders with the outcomes each role cares about most.

CMO · CMIO · Clinical Director

For Clinical Leaders

Structured post-discharge care plans and medication management keep patients on protocol between visits — reducing readmission risk without adding to your clinical team's workload. In-app messaging and telemedicine let your team resolve follow-up queries remotely, before they become readmissions.

CIO · CTO · VP Applications

For IT Leaders

Integrates with your existing HIS and EMR through standard HL7 and FHIR R4 interfaces — no rip-and-replace required. Modular architecture, provider-controlled data governance, and Zero Trust security give your IT team confidence from day one. Deploys on cloud or on-premise.

CFO · VP Operations · Administrator

For Administrators

Fewer no-shows, lower call-centre volume, and reduced per-patient administrative cost. Virtual queuing shortens wait times without adding front-desk headcount. The in-app marketplace and digital billing create new revenue streams while cutting collection cycles.
