About adeahub

adeahub is a digital health platform that helps hospitals, health systems, and national health authorities deliver personalised care at scale. Its four solutions — AH Engage, adeahub providers, adeahub connect, and adeahub insight — work together to unify clinical workflows, connect disparate systems, and surface the data clinicians need to act. With more than 500+ healthcare facilities and 50 million patient records across its installed base, and over 20 years of deployment experience in Asia, the Middle East, and Africa, adeahub powers modern healthcare where it is needed most.
